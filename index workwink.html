About us

A coach in your corner, not a careers portal.

WorkWink is an independent practice built for one moment that no one really prepares you for: the leap from studying or a long gap in employment into a career you actually care about.

No call centre, no script, no being passed between strangers. Just one coach who learns your situation and stays with you the whole way through. We take a holistic view — confidence and direction matter as much as a tidy CV — so the plan we build fits your life, not a template.

What we do

One subscription, the whole journey.

From the very first conversation to your first thirty days in the role, here's the path we walk together. Step in at the start — or jump in wherever you're stuck.

01

The first chat Free

A relaxed consultation to understand where you are, what's getting in the way, and where you actually want to go. No pressure, no commitment.

02

Find your direction

We narrow the noise into a shortlist of roles and industries that genuinely fit you — and a realistic plan to reach them.

03

Build the toolkit

A CV, profile and applications that actually get read. We sharpen how you tell your story so it lands with the people hiring.

04

Nail the interviews

Mock interviews, honest feedback and practical ways to handle nerves — so you walk in ready instead of rattled.

05

Offer & first 30 days

We help you weigh the offer, negotiate fairly and settle into the role — because landing the job is the start, not the finish line.
